Colter Wall: A Dark and Great Version of Country Music

Colter Wall's music takes us back to a version of country that we have not heard in a long while. It is gritty and dark all while still carrying a great tune and enjoyable beat. Colter Wall describes his music as "a mix of folk, blues, and Americana."

Many of his songs have also been used in movies, including Hell or High Water and Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri.
